About Me

Originally an aspiring performer, I found a niche in my life and passion, for coding. Living and growing in a rapidly advancing age of technology, I pursue a lifelong career and advancement in discovering the source of my everyday use of computer programming. Not unlike exploring the deep recesses of the sea, coding is a means of uncovering the origins of modern day programs and interfaces--each click of a computer mouse or the difference between an Apple or Windows operating system.

It is a rigorous journey to face, but we are not alone. Future programmers convene regularly to learn the trade of web development or other applicable means of code. It's one of the perks of enduring Rutgers Bootcamp.
